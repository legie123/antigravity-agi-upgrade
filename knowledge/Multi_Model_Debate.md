# 🏛️ Multi-Model Debate Protocol

Critical audits, implementation plans, and architectural decisions must undergo a parallel reasoning process.

## 1. The Debate Chain
To reach a final decision, a virtual consensus is generated between three distinct expertise paradigms:
1. **Claude (Safety & Integrity)**: Focuses on security, P&L safety, and system resilience.
2. **Llama (Architecture & Scale)**: Focuses on modularity, clean code, and scalability.
3. **DeepSeek (Performance & Runtime)**: Focuses on V8 performance, memory efficiency, and minimizing latency.

## 2. Mandatory Output Structure
Every audit or major plan must present:
- **Consensus**: What all models agree is perfect.
- **Clashes**: Where models disagree (e.g., Speed vs. Security).
- **Antigravity Verdict**: The final decision synthesized from the debate.

## 3. The Titan Engine
Use the `scripts/titan-debate.mjs` script to automate this process in any new environment.
